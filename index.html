How to use. Examples:

1st column are number representing power (population plus resources) - 40k population with 1 resource gives 5.
2st column is culture - it means province is building culture (developing or advancing).
Primitive country with 20k population builds development in 16 turns
With farms 1st column is 2/+1/+2 - it means province has 2 farms / and have one resource / and have two resources.
Primitive country with 20k population builds farm in 4 turns Primitive country with 20k population and one resource builds farm in 3 turns Primitive country with 20k population and two resources builds farm in 2 turns

I don't take more advanced forts into consideration
